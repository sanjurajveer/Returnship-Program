Our story

Why we built Re-Rise Her

The website was the result of a 3-month AWS She Builds program where Sanju Raj (mentee), under Joanna Wang (mentor), built the platform using AWS services. The program is designed to give women hands-on exposure to AWS and see what they can build and learn. During this journey, they also sought help from different solution architects to sort technical pieces. The idea and this website are direct outcomes of the She Builds program.
